### Previous Lessons (Still Valid)
1. **MAX 40% for "no event" outcomes** - never go above 40% for status quo
2. Include MORE diverse alternatives - the truth was often someone I didn't list
3. Search AGGRESSIVELY - check multiple news queries for each question
4. **Assume events WILL happen** - the simulation is designed so questions resolve with specific outcomes
5. Check Sky Sports, Al Jazeera, Strait Times - these sources had key information

### NEW CRITICAL RULES
6. **MARKET OPTIONS ARE A TRAP** - the market aggregate options are almost always WRONG. Use them only as a starting point, not as constraints.
7. **RESEARCH BEYOND MARKET** - for every question, search news to find the REAL possible answers. Names in articles are much more likely to be correct than names in market options.
8. **Include news-derived alternatives at 25-35%** even if they're not market options. This is where the wins come from.
9. **Connect related questions** - Pakistan boycott was about Bangladesh (same as Bangladesh cricket news). Morocco penalty was Brahim Diaz (mentioned as tournament's outstanding player with 5 goals).
