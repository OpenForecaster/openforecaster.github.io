## Key Lessons
1. **Don't assume the status quo continues**: For "who will be appointed" questions, the answer is someone NEW
2. **Consider all demographics**: For sports questions, consider both male and female athletes
3. **Broaden prediction spreads**: For highly uncertain questions, include more obscure options
4. **Don't over-concentrate probability**: Putting 80% on one option leads to big losses (Arizona 80% for measles!)
5. **Research more deeply**: Search specifically for the topic, not just general news
6. **Consider INSIDERS for political appointments**: Regime insiders (VPs, ministers), not just opposition
7. **The correct answer often comes from within the system**: Don't only look at external candidates
8. **AFCON bracket analysis is crucial**: Understanding exact bracket paths eliminates impossible matchups
